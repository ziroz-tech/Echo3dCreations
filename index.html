About E3C

Welcome to Echo 3D Creations! Based in Japan, Echo 3D Creations is a premier 3D game modeling and indie game development studio. With extensive experience in the industry, we specialize in creating captivating 3D designs that bring imaginative worlds to life.

At Echo 3D Creations, our expertise lies in crafting detailed background models, character models, and props. Our work is characterized by a strong attention to detail and a deep appreciation for Japanese aesthetics. We have a proven track record of producing high-quality assets for a variety of popular titles.
